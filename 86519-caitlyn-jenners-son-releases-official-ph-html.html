    24 Jul 2024, 00:00

    Three days after a very intimate wedding celebrated on the island of Nihi Sumba, Indonesia, Brody Jenner released the first official photo as a married couple with Kaitlynn Carter.

    On June 4, the 34-year-old son of Caitlyn Jenner and Linda Thompson took to Instagram to share a romantic photo of him holding his 29-year-old wife in the middle of an idyllic spot.

    'Well, this happened over the weekend,’ read Brody’s caption. In the photo, Brody looks great wearing a light beige suit with a white shirt. Carter looks happy in her long-sleeved white dress. Both are barefoot.

    As Daily Mail reported, Brody’s 58-year-old father Cailtyn had a previous commitment that date that forced her to be in Austria. TMZ, on the other hand, claims that family problems are the real reason for her absence.

    The fact that none of his half-sisters Kendall and Kylie Jenner didn’t attend his wedding either, further supports the rumors about a deep family divide caused by Caitlyn’s relationship with ex-wife Kris Jenner

    Despite TMZ claims that Brody was deeply hurt for Caitlyn not showing up at the wedding, he seems to be in heaven about formalizing his relationship with his girlfriend of four years and was very grateful for the experience.

    'Just want to thank @nihisumba and all of the amazing staff for creating one of the most incredible and memorable experiences of my life, Brody wrote on his Instagram, mentioning the resort where the couple and the guest stayed.

    ‘You guys truly deserve being the #1 resort in the world the past 2 years. Thanks to all my friends and family that came out to celebrate with @kaitlynn and I. Love you all. Till next year!!' Brody added next to a video of the resort.

    'Parting is such sweet sorrow,' expressed Carter about leaving the very place where Brody proposed her in 2016. About the reception, the newlywed shared: 'It was the most dreamy, incredible night ever.'

    Before the official ceremony, the festivities had lasted for days. His mother and his brother Brandon did attend the celebration. Read more on our Twitter account @amomama_usa.
