    The net worth of Hayden Voss

    Hayden Voss was born on June 8, 1993, in Marlborough, Massachusetts USA. He is a reality TV star known for his appearance in the 16th season of the reality TV show “Big Brother” in 2014. His net worth is estimated to be over $100,000 as of late 2017, mainly from his successful debut on reality TV and other work.

    Details about Hayden’s early life, upbringing, and education are not widely known as he has kept his childhood private from the media. Before joining the “Big Brother” house, Hayden worked as a pedicab driver on the streets of Los Angeles, California. He auditioned for the 16th installment of “Big Brother” in 2014 and appeared in 22 episodes before being evicted from the house, making history as the first male competitor not to be evicted unanimously.

    During his time in the “Big Brother” house, Hayden was part of the Bomb Squad alongside other contestants, including Amber Borzotra, Caleb Reynolds, and Cody Calafiore. He was also romantically involved with fellow contestant Nicole Franzel.

    After his stint on the show, Hayden withdrew from the public eye, even deleting his Twitter account for unspecified reasons. Since 2016, he has not made any public appearances.

    Hayden has successfully kept his personal life details away from the public eye.
