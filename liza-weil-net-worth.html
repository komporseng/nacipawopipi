    Liza Weil Wiki Biography

    Liza Weil was born on the 5th June 1977 in Passaic, New Jersey, USA of Jewish origin, and is an actress perhaps best known for her role of Paris Geller in the television series “Gilmore Girls” (2000 – 2007) and that of Bonnie Winterbottom in the television series “How to Get Away with Murder” (2014 – present). Weil has been active in the entertainment industry since 1983.
