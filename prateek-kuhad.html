    Early Childhood

    Career and Professional Life

    While making songs, Prateek started performing at local gigs in New York. His primary instrument is acoustic guitar however, he also plays piano and electric guitar. Later, he returned to India and released his first EP (Extended Play): Raat Raazi in 2013.

    Likewise, in 2015, he released his first full-length In Tokens & Charms. This album received the Indie Album of the Year 2015 by iTunes. In 2016, Kuhad worked on the song Kho Gaye Hum Kahaan for the Bollywood movie Baar Baar Dekho.

    It was his Bollywood debut as a singer and songwriter. The same year, he collaborated with Ankur Tewari to release the song Dil Beparwah for MTV India’s The Dewarists. Moreover, Prateek won the MTV Europe Music Award for Best Indian Act in 2016.

    Likewise, he won first place in the 2016 International Songwriting Competition. Later, he was awarded the Best Pop Artist Award at the Radio City Freedom Awards in 2018.

    Caption: Prateek Kuhad in one of his stage performances. Source: Facebook

    In 2018, he released the song Cold/Mess which was a roaring success. The song was included in former US President Barack Obama’s Favorite Music of 2019 list. This song also won the Indie Album of the Year 2019 by iTunes and Spotify: Top Indian Hits.

    In 2020, Kuhad became the first Indian to get signed by the American record label, Elektra Records. He releases his music in both Hindi and English. Furthermore, he has been featured in several magazines including Hindustan Times Brunch and GQ.
