    Tony Romo and Candice Crawford had a lot going for them in their careers before they crossed paths. A retired NFL quarterback who played for the Dallas Cowboys, Romo stood out on the field with four Pro Bowl nods in his 14-year career. In contrast, Candice Crawford, a former beauty pageant titleholder, chose a collaborative asset in journalism.  

    How They Met, Started Dating & Moved Into A Relationship

    The two met when Candice was completing an internship with the Dallas Cowboys. The two reportedly began dating in 2009 and married only two years later, in June 2011. Their love evolved through the years, and Tony would praise Candice for being so mindful of his football obligations because she stepped up as a great mother and wife.

    Difficulties and Hurdles as a High-Profile Public Couple

    Tony and Candice were a high-profile couple facing the trials of keeping their relationship intact under public scrutiny. Candice's work as a sports journalist, paired with Tony's NFL stardom, kept the couple in front of media cameras and tabloids. Of course, the pressures of Tony's athletic career and Candice's own media presence likely brought their fair share of demands to bear on a potential marriage.

    The Growing Family

    The Romo family grew with three boys coming to town, as Hawkins, Rivers, and Jones arrived were born. For them, the greatest milestones that occurred in their marriage was when they brought children into this world. Every major step in their family life has formed the solid, loving bond they maintain today.

    Building and Strengthening the Love

    Tony and his wife Candice have presented the picture of a happy couple in their public personae despite having two demanding careers. The way they can lean on one another, especially during Tony's pivotal transition from NFL quarterback to commentator, proves even more that those trying times were nothing compared to their commitment and loyalty.

    The tale of Tony Romo and Candice Crawford is as old as time. Love in the face of public scrutiny coupled with two people each breaking their neck to build an empire for themselves stands out among many.
