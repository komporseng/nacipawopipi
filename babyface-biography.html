    Who is Babyface?

    Babyface is an American singer, songwriter, and producer. He is popular for his albums Lovers (1986) Tender Lover (1989) For the Cool in You (1993), and more.

    Further for his amazing work, he has won a total of 12 Grammys.

    Babyface- Age, Parents, Siblings, Ethnicity, Education

    His birthdate is 10 April 1959 and was born in Indianapolis, Indiana, the U.S. as Kenneth Brian Edmonds. The name of his mother was Barbara Edmonds and his father’s name was Marvin Edmonds.

    Further, Babyface’s ancestry is All-American, and has two brothers named Kevon plus Melvin. He has other three siblings.

    As per his academics, he went to North Central High School. Indiana University Bloomington gave him an Honorary Doctorate degree in 2018.

    Babyface- Professional Life, Career

    Babyface’s first studio album titled Total Lovers came in 1986. His other hit albums are Tender Lover (1989), For the Cool in You (1993), The Day (1996), Face2Face (2001), Grown & Sexy (2005), Playlist (2007), and Return of the Tender Lover (2015).

    As a lead artist, he wrote tracks named “All Night Long”, “And I Love You”, “A Girl Like You”, “Bedtime”, “Breathe Again”, “Care for Me”, “Drama, Love & ‘Lationships”, “Girl in the Life Magazine”, “How Can U Be Down”, “Young Girl”, etc.

    Lil was also teamed up with other artists like Manchild, The Deele, and Toni Braxton.
