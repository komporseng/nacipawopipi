    Aviana Olea Le Gallo is a celebrity child best known as the only daughter of Superstar actress Amy Adams and her current husband, the artist Darren Le Gallo. Being the child of such a talented American actress and an acclaimed artist, there is no question that individuals are eager to know more about "Avi," as her family calls her.

    Aviana was born on May 15, 2010, to Amy Adams and her then-fiance, Darren. Her place of birth is in California, America, but she has European Ancestry from her parents, especially her father, who has French and German background.

    Aviana Has Already Published A Book

    When Aviana was just ten years old, she wrote a children's book called Dinosaur Princess. Her father, the artist, illustrated the book, and her famous mother promoted it. It has been selling quite well because of quality content & her parent's publicity. 

    Aviana has definitely inherited her parents' talent, and with her book already published, she is already showing her potential. The young girl is definitely climbing it to the top.

    A Quick Tour Of Aviana Mother's Personal Life

    Aviana's mom, Amy Adams, was born in Italy on August 20, 1974, as Amy Lou Adams to American parents. Her father, Richard Adams, was in the military, and her other Kathryn was into bodybuilding and later ran a gym. Amy was very athletic at an early age and did track and gymnastics. She later went to follow dance as a passion and took up acting.

    Amy chose not to go to college and took up waitressing jobs and theatre gigs. She worked hard to make it as an actress but did not have much success till she was in her thirties. Her movie Junebug shot her to fame, and her role as Ella in Enchanted made her a household name. 

    Junebug actress Amy has natural strawberry blonde hair but to get more roles and differ as an actress, she colored her hair to deep red. She has stunning blue eyes and a beautiful slim rectangle body figure.

    Regarding Amy's family, she has four brothers and two sisters from her parents. They were raised Mormons but later exited the church. Amy has stated that her family was not rich and struggled, but the family was full of love for each other. 

    Who Is Aviana Olea Le Gallo's Father? 

    Aviana is the daughter of German-origin American citizen Darren Le Gallo, who has worked as an actor and an artist. He was born on July 21, 1974, to a German mother and a French father. The family moved to the USA when he was a teenager. Darren identifies as a Christian. 

    He graduated from the Albiene Christian School and later obtained a Bachelor of Arts with a major in painting. After finishing his education, he then moved to sunny Los Angeles and worked as an actor. Later he worked in various art galleries and became the primary caretaker of his daughter. 

    Crazy Night actor Darren has even illustrated a children's book. Recently, he wrote and directed a movie named Sam and Kate, which was released on veterans day. Darren has also released some abstract electric music tracks.

    How Rich Are Aviana Olea Le Gallo's Parents? 

    The net worth of Amy Adams is estimated to be around $40 million, similar to American actress Rene Russo, with Amy earning about $15 million a year. Her career success is quite inspirational because she did not achieve fame until she was in her thirties. Since then, she has gone on to be one of the highest-paid actresses in Hollywood.

    On the other hand, Darren Le Gallo is also quite successful and is estimated to be worth about $2 million. His art career is quite successful, and he has worked with some of the most prestigious galleries in America. 

    How Did Aviana's Parents Get Hitched?

    Aviana Olea Le Gallo's parents, Amy Adams and Darren Le Gallo, dated for fourteen years before tying the knot in a grand ceremony on 2 May 2015. They got engaged in 2008 and had their daughter two years later in 2010.

    According to sources, Amy and Darren hastened their nuptials at their daughter's request. She wanted them to be married, and they were more than willing to comply.

    How Did Aviana's Parents Meet? 

    Reports suggest that Aviana's parents, Amy and Darren met when they were both in the same acting class in 2001. They were both involved with other people but stayed friends for about a year. After Amy's recent breakup, Darren asked her out; the rest is history.

    The pair hit it off and have been together for years. More importantly, they share a great bond. With Amy off to take her high-paying projects, Darren has been amazingly supportive and protective of the actress.

    Grandparents Of Aviana Olea De Gallo

    Aviana's grandparents, through her mother, are Richard Adams and his ex-wife, Kathryn Adams. The two parents had seven children together, three girls and four boys. They are Americans who used to be members of the Mormon Church. 

    Amy's parents raised their kids in Vicenza, Italy, for their early years and later returned to Castle Rock, Colorado. They split in 1985, and Richard left to remarry while Kathryn raised the kids.

    Darren's parents, on the other hand, stay away from publicity. Not much is known about them, but he is reported as their only child. It seems that he doesn't want to disturb his parent's personal life. 

    When Was Aviana's First Public Appearance?

    Since then, her public appearances have been very sparse. Her family does show her off on rare Instagram posts, the most recent ones being the one wishing her a happy eleventh birthday and another celebrating Father's day.
