    David Bederman Wiki, Age & Family

    Bederman is 37 years old now. He was born in the year 1986 and blows out his birthday candle in the month of May.

    Additionally, he goes by his full name David Samuel Bederman. He hails from Potomac, Maryland, and belongs to American nationality.

    David was raised by his parents Andrew Bederman and Jill Spector Bederman. His father Andy is also an attorney by profession and the co-founder of Greenberg & Bederman, LLC. He is a graduate of the University of Wisconsin-Madison and Case Western Reserve University School of Law.

    David Bederman in a family picture with his parents and a sister. (Source: Andy Bederman’s Facebook)

    Similarly, his mother Jill is a former reading teacher at Montgomery Co. Public Schools. She is a 1975 graduate of the University of Wisconsin-Madison.

    The youngest of two children, he is accompanied by a sister named Sarah Miriam Bederman. Sarah serves as an Online Support Group Facilitator at Postpartum Support International. Moreover, she is a MA Student in Clinical Psychology at Pepperdine University Graduate School of Education and Psychology.

    His Education Details

    Bederman pursued his high school education in his hometown. He then attended Northwestern University where he graduated with a Bachelor of Arts in Psychology and International Studies in 2008.

    Afterward, he went to Loyola Marymount University’s Loyola Law School in Los Angeles, California. There, he graduated cum laude with a Juris Doctor in 2012 and was selected to the Order of the Coif. Additionally, he also worked as Loyola Law Review Senior Technical Editor at college.

    What Is David Bederman’s Job?

    Bederman is an attorney by profession with a decade of experience in the field. He is licensed to practice law by the State Bar of California. As of now, he serves as an attorney at Abir Cohen Treyzon Salo, LLP, a law firm in San Diego, California.

    Prior to that, he worked at another law firm, Manning & Kass, Ellrod, Ramirez, and Trester LLP from 2016 to 2017. Likewise, he has worked as an attorney at Kaiser Gornick LLP and Waters & Kraus LLP.   David also served as a judicial extern for the U.S. District Court, and Central District of California in Los Angeles.
