    Jhansi Laxmi – Education

    Moving towards her education details, Jhansi completed her schooling at Kendriya Vidhyala No. 1, Golconda. Later, she joined the VIT Vellore College and graduated in chemical engineering.

    Jhansi Laxmi – Professional Life and Career

    Host Career

    Jhansi Laxmi started her career as a host in the TV show “Talk of the Town” in 1997. The show aired for almost a decade between 1997 to 2007 on Gemini TV. Jhansi worked till the end episode of the show and got a big breakthrough in her hosting career.

    Along with this, Jhansi has hosted many other shows such as “Black”, “Naveena”, “ATM”, “Pelli Pustalam”, “Ko Ante Koti”, and “Sunday Sandadi”. Additionally, Jhansi hosted the first, second, and third seasons of the TV show “Black” which is the first Indian reality show for visually impaired people.

    Acting Career

    Jhansi Laxmi made her acting debut with the movie “Aahavanam” in 1997. After the huge success of the film, she appeared in many other movies. Some of them are “Jayam Mandera” (2000), “Family Circus” (2001), “Sontham” (2002), “Yagnam” (2004), “Bhadra” (2005), “Sri Krishna” (2006), “Pellaina KOthalo” (2006),  “Ashta Chamma-Heroine Aunt” (2008), ‘”Racha” (2012), ‘”Saakshyam” (2018), “Maharshi” (2019), “Kousalya Krishnamurthy” (2019), “Pressure Cooker” (2020), and “F3” (2021).

    Along with this, Jhansi made her debut as a director and playwright in theatres with the play “Purusha Sooktam” at Ravindra Bharati in 2019.
