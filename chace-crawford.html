    Career and Professional Life

    Chace Crawford is an American actor best known for his role as Nate Archibald on The CW’s teen drama series Gossip Girl. He made his acting debut in 2006 in the thriller television film Long Lost Son as Matthew Williams / Mark Halloran.

    He made his debut in the film industry in the same year. Crawford appeared in the horror film The Covenant. Renny Harlin directed the film and it features Steven Strait, Taylor Kitsch, Toby Hemingway, Sebastian Stan, Jessica Lucas, and Laura Ramsey alongside Crawford.

    He played the character of Tyler Simms.

    Around this time, he got the opportunity to play what would become his most successful role to date. He was signed for the drama television series Gossip Girl. 

    He portrayed one of the lead male characters of the show and the boyfriend of one of the lead female characters. His character was also known as “the golden boy of Manhattan’s Upper East Side.”

    Additionally, he was part of the show for 115 episodes between 2007 and 2012. He received numerous nominations and awards for his performance in the series.

    After appearing in Gossip Girl, he appeared in several films like The Haunting of Molly Hartley in 2008, Twelve in 2010, and Peace, Love & Misunderstanding in 2011.

    2012 onwards

    In 2012, he was cast as ‘Marco’ in the romantic film What to Expect When You’re Expecting. Kirk Jones directed the film which features artists like Cameron Diaz, Jennifer Lopez, Elizabeth Banks, Brooklyn Decker, Anna Kendrick, Matthew Morrison, Dennis Quaid, and Chris Rock.

    The film revolves around five couples who are expecting their first baby. Crawford acted opposite Anna Kendrick in the film. He was nominated at the Teen Choice Awards for Choice Movie: Male Scene Stealer after impressing everyone with his work in the film.

    His other film credits include Rules Don’t Apply, Eloise, and I Do… Until I Don’t, All About Nina, and Charlie Says. Crawford appeared in the prime-time television soap opera Blood & Oil, created by Rodes Fishburne and Josh Pate.

    He appeared as Billy LeFever in ten episodes in 2015 and earned a nomination at the People’s Choice Awards for Favorite Actor in a New TV Series the next year.

    Beyond acting, he is also a handsome bachelor in the Hollywood entertainment industry. He was the new face of Diet Coke in 2012. Then Glamour magazine ranked him as the #12 Sexiest Man in 2011.
